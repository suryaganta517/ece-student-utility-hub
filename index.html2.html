Binary ↔ Decimal Converter

Convert numbers between base-2 (binary) and base-10 (decimal) number systems instantly.

📖 Concept

Binary (Base-2) is the language of computers — every piece of data is stored as a sequence of 0s and 1s. Decimal (Base-10) is the human number system. Understanding conversion is fundamental to Computer Science, Digital Electronics, and Networking.

λ Binary: Each position = 2ⁿ | E.g., 1010₂ = 1×2³ + 0×2² + 1×2¹ + 0×2⁰ = 10₁₀

Calculate your Cumulative Grade Point Average with weighted credit system.

📖 Concept

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) measures your overall academic performance on a 10-point scale. Each subject has a credit weight that reflects its importance. Higher-credit subjects affect your CGPA more.

CGPA = Σ(Grade Points × Credits) ÷ Σ(Credits) | % ≈ CGPA × 9.5

Antenna Wavelength Calculator

Calculate electromagnetic wavelength from frequency using the speed of light.

📖 Concept

Wavelength (λ) is the physical length of one complete cycle of an electromagnetic wave. Antenna engineers design antennas to be exactly λ/2 or λ/4 in length for maximum efficiency. Higher frequencies have shorter wavelengths.

λ = c / f | c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s | Antenna = λ/2 or λ/4

Resistor Color Code

Decode 4-band resistor color codes to find resistance value and tolerance.

📖 Concept

Resistors use a color-band system to display their value since the components are too small for printed text. Each color represents a digit (0–9). The 3rd band is a multiplier and the 4th band shows tolerance. Mnemonics like "BB ROY of Great Britain has Very Good Wife" help remember the order.

R = (Band1 × 10 + Band2) × Multiplier ± Tolerance%

Attendance Calculator

Track attendance percentage and know exactly how many classes you can safely skip.

📖 Concept

Most universities mandate a minimum 75% attendance to be eligible for exams. Falling below this can result in detention or debarment. This calculator shows your current standing and gives you a precise skip-budget or catch-up plan.

Attendance % = (Classes Attended / Total Classes) × 100 | Min 75%

Engineering Unit Converter

Convert between dBm and Watts, and between frequency units used in RF engineering.

📖 Concept

dBm (decibel-milliwatt) is a logarithmic power unit referenced to 1 mW. It's widely used in RF, wireless, and telecom because it can represent huge power ranges compactly. 0 dBm = 1 mW, 30 dBm = 1 W.

dBm = 10 × log₁₀(P_mW) | P_W = 10^(dBm/10) / 1000

Pomodoro Study Timer

The Pomodoro Technique: 25 minutes of deep focus followed by a 5-minute break.

📖 Concept

Developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s, the Pomodoro Technique is a time-management method that improves focus and reduces mental fatigue. Work is broken into 25-minute intervals (pomodoros), separated by short breaks. After 4 sessions, take a longer 15–30 minute break.

1 Pomodoro = 25 min Work + 5 min Break | Every 4th = 15–30 min Break
